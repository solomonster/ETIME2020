More Information on ETIME-FOUNDATION

Methodology

Mathematics has become the basic ingredients for the development of mankind. It was therefore accorded as “ILMU WAJIB” by leading Islamic countries like Turkey, Saudi Arabia, Pakistan, Iran, Egypt and the United Arab Emirates. The ETIME Foundation Mathematics program was built on the basis of discipline, dedication with a unique combination of team work, diligence, accountability and respect in addition to sincere commitment to legitimate endeavors (Halal) The key principles that drive our initiatives include Modularity, application of classical methods, individual instructions with its zero failure results and team/group work and its attendant blessings.
